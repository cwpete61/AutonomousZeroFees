# Campaign SOP: Launching the Marketing Machine (Orbis Outreach - BPS)

The Orbis Outreach Marketing Machine is an autonomous engine designed to discover, audit, and engage high-value service businesses. This SOP outlines the professional workflow for launching and managing a high-performance outbound campaign.

---

## 🏗️ Phase 1: Market Intelligence
*Before launching, you must define the scope of your discovery.*

1.  **Identify the Niche**: Focus on U.S.-based service businesses (e.g., HVAC, Realtors, Dental) that typically have high ticket values but often underperform in digital visibility.
2.  **Define Geography**: Select targets by State or City to ensure proximity or regional relevance for localized outreach.
3.  **Set Lead Threshold**: Determine your target volume. A standard batch is 50 leads to maintain high quality and deliverability health.

---

## ✍️ Phase 2: Narrative Generation
*Establish the communication sequence that will represent your agency.*

1.  **Navigate to Email Sequences**: Go to the **EMAIL CAMPAIGNS** tab.
2.  **Create New Sequence**: Click `✉️ New Email Sequence`.
3.  **Deploy AutoWriteEmail™**: 
    - Enable the **AutoWriteEmail™** toggle.
    - Provide the **Industry**, **Pain Point** (e.g., "low website traffic"), and **Primary Outcome** (e.g., "2x lead volume").
    - **Pro Tip**: Select your preferred AI Engine (**Anthropic Claude** or **OpenAI GPT**) for generation.
4.  **Refine & Save**: Review the 3-5 step sequence generated by the AI. Ensure the "AUTO" badge is visible on each step, then save.

---

## 🚀 Phase 3: Launching the Autonomous Engine
*Executing the discovery and outreach campaign.*

1.  **Open Campaign Wizard**: Click `🚀 Launch Campaign` on the Dashboard.
2.  **Step 1: Identity**: Give your campaign a descriptive name (e.g., "Spring HVAC Recovery - Houston").
3.  **Step 2: Targeting**: Select your Niche and Geography.
4.  **Step 3: Optimization**: Select the Email Sequence created in Phase 2.
5.  **Step 4: Automation Logic**:
    - **Toggle AutoWrite Outreach**: When enabled, the AI will hyper-personalize the outreach for *each individual lead* discovered.
    - **Schedule**: Choose "Immediate Launch" to begin the Scout Agent's discovery process instantly.

---

## 🕵️ Phase 4: Operational Oversight
*Monitoring the machine in action.*

1.  **The Scout In Action**: Go to the **JOB QUEUE** tab. You will see the **Scout Agent** (Playwright-driven) beginning its search across search engines and directories.
2.  **Lead Scoring**: As leads are discovered, the machine automatically:
    - **Audits** the website for performance/SEO.
    - **Qualifies** based on maturity signals.
    - **Enriches** contact data (names, emails, LinkedIn).
3.  **Management Dashboard**: Monitor the **Pipeline** to see leads move from `DISCOVERED` to `QUALIFIED`.

---

## ✅ Phase 5: Approvals & Conversion
*Human-in-the-loop validation.*

1.  **Approval Requests**: Check the **Pipeline** for leads marked `OUTREACH_PENDING`.
2.  **Validation**: Review the AI-generated personalized email. Confirm it hits the industry context correctly.
3.  **Approve**: Click **Approve** to move the lead to `OUTREACH_SENT`. The system will now handle the automated follow-up delays (e.g., 0 days, 3 days, 7 days).
4.  **Reply Management**: Monitor the **Conversations** tab. The AI will classify replies as `Interested`, `Meeting Scheduled`, or `Unsubscribe`.

---

## 💡 Best Practices for Success

> [!TIP]
> **Domain Health**: Do not exceed 50 new outreach starts per domain per day to prevent spam flagging.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Context is King**: The quality of AI emails depends on the **Pain Point Signals** you provide in Phase 2. Use specific signals like "missing tracking pixels" or "404 page errors" for better engagement.

> [!CAUTION]
> **Master Sync**: Ensure the Dashboard is in "Sync Online" mode before launching a campaign to ensure all agent data persists correctly to the PostgreSQL cluster.
